#  Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

##  Do’s
- Keep your profile professional and updated  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content  
- Share useful and meaningful content  
- Be mindful of your digital footprint  

---

##  Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling  
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people  
- Don’t click on suspicious links or ads  
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- Don’t overshare your location or daily routine  
- Don’t use weak or repeated passwords  

---

##  Reminder
Your online presence reflects your personality and can impact your academic and professional opportunities. Always use social media responsibly.
