Do’s
1)Keep your profiles professional and updated
2)Think before posting anything online
3)Use strong passwords and enable 2-factor authentication
4)Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
5)Verify information before sharing
Don’ts
1)Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
2)Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
3)Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling
4)Don’t accept requests from unknown people blindly
5)Don’t click on suspicious links
