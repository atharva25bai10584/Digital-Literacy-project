#  Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

##  General Safety Tips
- Always check the sender’s email address carefully  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details  
- Use strong and unique passwords for each account  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities  
- Be cautious while downloading files from unknown sources  

---

##  Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Double-check UPI payment requests before approving  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes for payments  
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone  
- Verify payment requests even if they appear from known contacts  

---

##  Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: **1930** (for financial fraud cases)  

---

##  Reminder
Staying alert and informed is the best way to protect yourself from cybercrime. Small precautions can prevent major losses.
